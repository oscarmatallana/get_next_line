1. First: Test with Valgrind

cc -Wall -Wextra -Werror -D BUFFER_SIZE=42 \
get_next_line.c get_next_line_utils.c Line_Counter.c

valgrind --leak-check=full ./a.out "01. A"

It should say: All heap blocks were freed -- no leaks are possible

Repeat with:

-D BUFFER_SIZE=1
-D BUFFER_SIZE=10
-D BUFFER_SIZE=9999

2. Second: Tripouille's gnlTester

git clone https://github.com/Tripouille/gnlTester.git

cd gnlTester

Copy the files:

cp ../get_next_line.c .
cp ../get_next_line_utils.c .
cp ../get_next_line.h .

cp ../get_next_line_bonus.c .
cp ../get_next_line_utils_bonus.c .
cp ../get_next_line_bonus.h .

And then run:

make -j$(nproc) m

or:

make m

and make b for the bonus part.
