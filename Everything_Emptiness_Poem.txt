November 09/07 

Within a circle
there is another circle
which has a circle
inside it
vertiginous
it forms a large vortex
that at infinity
nothing must be
If a cut is made
after a cut
that was made
before a first
it comes to the very tiny
the very small
less than that
nothing must be
And beyond the smallest brick
is hidden the centre of all there is:
Emptiness constitutes everything.

- El Matallana
